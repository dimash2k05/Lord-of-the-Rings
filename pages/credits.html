Directors and producers

  • Directed by Peter Jackson
  • Screenplay by Fran Walsh, Philippa Boyens, Peter Jackson
  • Based on "The Lord of the Rings" by J.R.R. Tolkien
  • Produced by Barrie M. Osborne, Fran Walsh, Peter Jackson
  • Starring Elijah Wood, Ian McKellen, Liv Tyler, Viggo Mortensen, and others
  • Cinematography by Andrew Lesnie
  • Edited by John Gilbert, Michael Horton, Jamie Selkirk
  • Music by Howard Shore
  • Production companies: New Line Cinema, WingNut Films, The Saul Zaentz Company
  • Distributed by New Line Cinema
